reorderPartitioning(self)¶
We collect the information of how many cells f_{p,q} of which

partition q are on each subdomain p. Then we solve a linear program

max_n sum_{p=1}^P sum_{q=1}^Q f_{p,q} * n_{p,q} subject to sum_q n_{p,q} = 1

orall p = 1,..,P (each subdomain gets one partition)

sum_p n_{p,q} <= 1

orall q = 1,..,Q (each partition gets at most one subdomain)
